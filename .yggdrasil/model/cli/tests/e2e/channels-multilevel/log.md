Adds end-to-end coverage for rules that reach components several levels deep and for conditional applicability filters keyed on every kind of typed dependency. The guarantees: a rule attached high in the hierarchy reaches descendants several generations down and is attributed to the right ancestor; an applicability filter keyed on any dependency kind is evaluated against the receiving component's own dependencies; combined filters compose; and the alternate inheritance mode for rule-implies-rule edges behaves as specified. These are the paths most likely to break during a refactor of a deep architecture.
