Adds end-to-end coverage for the remaining ways an explicit, human-authored waiver is scoped and validated by the local programmable reviewers. A waiver must require a stated reason so the exception is never anonymous, must match the rule it names rather than waive blanket, must stay inert while the rule it targets is dormant and take effect once the rule activates, and must support waiving several named rules at once. These scoping and validation guarantees are what keep a waiver a deliberate, auditable exception instead of a hole in enforcement; the suite proves them against the real binary.
