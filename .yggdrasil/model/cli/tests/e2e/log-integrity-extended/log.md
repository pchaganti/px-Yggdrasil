Extends end-to-end coverage of the append-only per-node history guarantee. The history file is integrity-protected: its pre-baseline portion is hashed at approve time, so any later edit, deletion, reordering, or fabrication of entries must be detected and refused rather than silently accepted. This suite spawns the real binary and tampers with history in every distinguishable way — modifying historical content, deleting the file, appending out-of-order or malformed entries, corrupting format, and dropping or fabricating entries during a merge reconciliation — asserting each is caught with its documented reason. The motivation is that history integrity is a trust anchor: if tampering went undetected, the business rationale recorded for past decisions could be rewritten without trace, so the detection paths must be continuously proven.
